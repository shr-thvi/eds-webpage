About Us

The Echelon Dev Society is a community of developers, designers, and innovators from Chameli Devi Group of Institutions. We organize hackathons, coding events, and development projects to help students enhance their technical skills and creativity.


We’re not just another club – we’re a group of students who want to shake up the tech scene, bring fresh ideas to life, and have fun while doing it. Think of us as a launchpad for innovation, where you'll get the chance to work on real projects, work with like-minded people, and grow your skills beyond the classroom.

We believe in the power of hackathons, hands-on projects, and building things together to turn ideas into reality. But here’s the thing – we need you to help us make it happen! Whether you’re passionate about coding, organizing events, creating content, or just love the idea of building something cool with a team – we’ve got a spot for you.


What’s in it for you?

Joining the Echelon Dev Society isn’t just about being part of a club; it’s about leveling up:

  • Exclusive Member Kits: Get cool club-branded gear and essentials.
  • Industry Visits: Explore real-world tech environments and gain exposure.
  • Networking Opportunities: Work alongside industry experts, expand your connections, and learn from the best.
  • Hands-On Experience: Build projects, participate in hackathons, and polish your skills with guidance from peers and mentors.
